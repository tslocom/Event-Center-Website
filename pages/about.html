About Vegas Expo Center

The Vegas Expo Center has 4.65 million square feet of meeting space, spanning 74 meeting rooms, the largest of which being a 2.1 million square foot exhibit space.

We have a full ballroom, 35 confrence rooms, 24 auditoriums, and 15 exhibit halls. A fully staffed kitchen also takes care of all of our guests catering needs, even taking custom requests.

Our Mission

Our mission at the Vegas Expo Center is to provide a world-class venue for events, conferences, and exhibitions, fostering innovation, collaboration, and memorable experiences for all attendees.

Our History

Established in 1915, the Vegas Expo Center started as a modest exhibition hall with just a few rooms, and has since evolved into one of the largest and most versatile event venues in the world.

Over the decades, it has hosted countless significant events, from trade shows to international conferences, contributing to Las Vegas's reputation as a premier destination for business and entertainment.
